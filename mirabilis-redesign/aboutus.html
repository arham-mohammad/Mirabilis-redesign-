About us

This is our story

We’ve been there, buying your first home is difficult. That’s why we created Mirabilis Homes. Our goal at Mirabilis is to make the homeownership journey as easy as possible by eliminating the key affordability challenges – debt and down payment – and reduce friction throughout the process. With our program, you can become a homeowner in as little as two weeks!

Our Purpose

Why Mirabilis Homes?

Our INTRO Program is designed to enable our clients to buy the first home while maintaining financial flexibility. We are your partners during this process, literally, and are thus committed to helping you achieve financial freedom and the dream of owning your home.

What we do?

Mirabilis Homes offers an alternative solution to the traditional way of buying a home. Our INTRO program allows our clients to become homeowners without debt or a significant down payment. Through our co-ownership method, we can drastically reduce the barriers of entry to home buying compared to traditional ways and allow our clients to follow a home buying plan that’s best suited for their needs. At Mirabilis, we’re changing the way people buy their homes, for the better.

Vision

To reduce the wealth gap and increase financial freedom by providing tools, resources, and thoughtful innovations that provide access to homeownership for everyone.

Our Mission

To facilitate the homeownership dream for everyone.

Our Value

Our clients are our partners, literally. We work closely with our partners to find the best possible solution. We understand everyone’s case is different and are committed to doing our best to help you achieve your financial goals.
